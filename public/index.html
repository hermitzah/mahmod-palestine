Mahmoud's story

Palestine · Father of three

Mahmoud Al-Hassan, 34, once a schoolteacher in Gaza, lost his home, his classroom, and nearly everything in the recent escalation. Living in a temporary tent with his children and elderly mother, he struggles daily to find clean water, bread, and medication for his youngest daughter, Lina, who suffers from asthma.

Despite the devastation, Mahmoud refuses to lose hope. "I want my children to dream again — to go back to school, to feel safe," he says. Your donation directly provides emergency aid, hygiene kits, warm meals, and critical medical support.
